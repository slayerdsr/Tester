Our Story

A Little Bakery with a Big Heart

Nestled in the heart of Banashankari Stage II, Cocoa Bakes has become Bengaluru's beloved neighbourhood bakery — crafting handmade pastries, cakes, pizzas, and comfort food for everyone.

With over 1,000 five-star reviews and a loyal community of sweet-toothed locals, we pour passion into every recipe. From our legendary Chocolate Truffle Cake to our savoury Dragon Paneer, there's always something new to discover.
